about me !

1999 · Ali's cosmic home

hi, i’m ali. this is my corner of the net. i dump notes from whatever books & articles i’m reading then try to turn the mess into little posts so future-me (and maybe you) can find them again. it wanders into politics, sociology, pop stuff, philosophy plus the odd story that has no business being as funny as it was at the time.

some folks would call a chunk of this 'woke propaganda'. that’s fine. bring snacks.

i’m doing a phd but these posts aren’t my dissertation. sometimes i get things wrong. sometimes i fix them.

the aim is a couple of updates a month (never more than once a day my brain needs a brake). topics will hop around because mine do :D

say hi if you feel like it and i hope you enjoy your stay!!


fiat lux!
