1999 · Ali's cosmic home

hi, i’m ali. this is my corner of the net. i dump notes from whatever books & articles i’m tangled up in, then try to turn the mess into little posts so future-me (and maybe you) can find them again. it wanders: politics, sociology, pop stuff, philosophy, plus the odd story that has no business being as funny as it was at the time.

 

some folks would call a chunk of this “woke propaganda.” that’s fine. bring snacks.



i’m not your professor. yes i’m doing a phd; no, these posts aren’t my dissertation. sometimes i get things wrong. sometimes i fix them.

 

 

aim is a couple of updates a week (never more than once a day; brain needs a brake). topics will hop around because mine do.

 

say hi if you feel like it.
