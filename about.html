Hey there! Welcome to my blog! My name is Ali. I started this blog to document what I was learning from the books and articles I happen(ed) to be reading. After reflecting on my notes, I decided to organise my writings into semi-structured posts with the hope that others might benefit from them. These include topics in politics, sociology, pop culture, philosophy, and even lighthearted personal stories. A large part of my writing is what the Righties might call “woke propaganda.”

I am not an expert or academic. Despite pursuing doctoral studies, none of my posts have anything to do with my PhD.

Anyway, I've tried to spruce up the blog to make it a cosy spot for anyone who wanders by. My ideal is to update at least twice a week and no more than once a day, and I’ll try to vary up the topics as much as I can.

Don't hesitate to reach out.

Fiat lux!
