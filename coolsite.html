Academic:

  • New Left Review: High-imact factor leftist Pol Sci. magazine.
  • E-International Relations: Short articles on IR. Can be technical.
  • Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: The main Philosophy wiki.
  • Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Another good philosophy wiki.
  • LessWrong: Philosophy, AI, Psychology, and Econ. Notably contrarian.
  • Astral Codex Ten: Psychology and philosophy. Contrarian.
  • RationalWiki: Wiki for skeptics. Noteworthy article: Just asking questions.
  • Lance Independent: Philosophy; metaethics-centric and accessible.
  • PEA Soup: Accessible philosophy blog with short posts.
  • Robert Brown: Physics and Philosophy professor's blog.
  • London Review of Books: Well-known literary magazine for book reviews and essays.
  • The New York Review of Books: Another notable literary magazine.
  • Philosophy Magazine: Accessible philosophy articles. Rare gem
  • Social Epistemology Review: Academic blog on social epistemology for general readership.

Retro Webmaster Tools:

  • GIF cities: Hundreds of classic gifs.
  • Melon Land: Classic gifs, textures, pets and other tools.
  • Cursor effects: Adds cool 90s cursor effects to your cursor.
  • Geocities: Ideas and gifs.
  • Nineties: 90s aesthetic html ideas.
  • Git & GitHub Guide: A comprehensive guide on Git and GitHub.

Nostalgia Trips:

  • PCJS: Uses javascript to simulate classic computer hardware from web 1.0. What a time to be alive.
  • Old Web Today: Relive the old internet days with classic browsers.
  • The Old Net: Vintage web browsing.
  • Wiby: A search engine that only indexes Web.10 websites.
  • Old Computers: Musuem of classic computers and internet.
  • Geocities Restorative: Visit blogs last updated in the 90s and 2000s.
  • My90sTV: 90s TV simulator.
  • My80sTV: A nostalgic dive into 80s TV shows and commercials.
  • Wiby Surprise: Click to send you to a random old webpage. Each click is a surprise.
  • Flash Musuem: Archive of thousands of old flash games.
